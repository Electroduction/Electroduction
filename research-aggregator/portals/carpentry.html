Introduction to Carpentry

Carpentry is the skilled trade of cutting, shaping, and installing building materials during construction.

Types of Carpentry

  • Rough Carpentry - Framing, structural work
  • Finish Carpentry - Trim, molding, cabinetry
  • Cabinetmaking - Custom cabinets and furniture
  • Formwork - Concrete forms

Essential Tools

  • Measuring - Tape measure, speed square, level
  • Cutting - Circular saw, miter saw, hand saw
  • Fastening - Hammer, nail gun, drill
  • Layout - Chalk line, laser level
