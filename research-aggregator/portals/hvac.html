Introduction to HVAC

HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) systems control indoor climate and air quality.

HVAC Components

  • Heating - Furnaces, boilers, heat pumps
  • Cooling - Air conditioners, chillers, evaporative coolers
  • Ventilation - Air exchange, ductwork, exhaust fans
  • Refrigeration - Commercial cooling systems

The Refrigeration Cycle

The refrigeration cycle transfers heat using compression and expansion of refrigerant.
