Introduction to Art & Design

Design is the intentional arrangement of elements to create visual communication and solve problems.

Design Disciplines

  • Graphic Design - Visual communication for print and digital
  • UX/UI Design - User experience and interface design
  • Industrial Design - Physical product design
  • Motion Design - Animation and video graphics
  • Typography - The art of arranging type

Principles of Design

  • Balance - Visual weight distribution
  • Contrast - Difference between elements
  • Hierarchy - Order of importance
  • Repetition - Consistent elements throughout
  • Proximity - Grouping related elements
  • White Space - Breathing room in design
