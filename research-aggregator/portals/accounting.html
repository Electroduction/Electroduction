Introduction to Accounting

Accounting is the systematic recording, analyzing, and reporting of financial transactions. It provides the financial information needed for business decisions.

Branches of Accounting

  • Financial Accounting - External reporting per GAAP/IFRS
  • Managerial Accounting - Internal decision-making
  • Tax Accounting - Tax compliance and planning
  • Auditing - Independent verification
  • Forensic Accounting - Fraud investigation

The Accounting Equation

Assets = Liabilities + Equity

This equation must ALWAYS balance.
Every transaction affects at least two accounts.
